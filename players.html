Players

The Order of Braylon

Named by the first king of Kavaya, this group of witches played the role of councilors to the kings through the years. When a new king was crowned, he had the authority to reorganize The Order as he saw fit. Even though Braylon is long dead, the Order of Braylon serves whatever king sits on the throne.

The Witches of Aldarath

When Azreal demoted the Order of Braylon, he had a new group waiting to take it's place. He gave each member of the new group a pocket mirror. After learning the fantastical history of these mirrors, they chose to carry on the name of mirrors' creator. They are The Witches of Aldarath.
