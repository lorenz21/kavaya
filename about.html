Story

Tales of Kavaya is an exciting adventure about the most powerful witches in the land. Join Azreal, Grey, Morgan, Ayari, and Rowan as they replace a generation of rulers. Through the ups and downs of leadership, the five witches experience firsthand the many struggles that come with their new roles, all while juggling their personal lives.

Warnings

Tales of Kavaya is a webcomic featuring mature content and may not be suitable for underage children. With that, we the creators feel it is necessary to provide trigger warnings for our readers. Please note the following items as you will come across them while exploring Kavaya:

  • Swearing
  • Attempted Rape
  • Abuse (physical, emotional, sexual)
  • Child Abuse/ Pedophilia
  • Self-harm

We have happy stuff, too. We promise!
