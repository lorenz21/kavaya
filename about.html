Story

As our world ends, a new one begins: one reshaped by the forces that be. In this new world, humanity gets a second chance. A millenia has past and once again,

Tales of Kavaya is an exciting adventure about the most powerful witches in the land. Join Azreal, Grey, Morgan, Ayari, and Rowan as they replace a generation of rulers. Through the ups and downs of leadership, the five witches experience firsthand the many struggles that come with their new roles, all while juggling their personal lives.
